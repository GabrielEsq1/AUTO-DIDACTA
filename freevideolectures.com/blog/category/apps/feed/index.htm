]]> Envious of mates who utter fancy words while chatting or colleagues who articulate crisp and fresh sentences at the presentation meeting! Vocabulary becomes particularly important if you are appearing for exams like CAT or TOEFL, or you need to impress your bosses. Now improve your vocabulary with a mobile phone as we present you a list of vocabulary improving apps for Android phones.

1. PowerVocab: Vocab Word Game

Choose an appropriate meaning for a word by taking ‘Word Challenges’. Every right answer will unlock additional words while the app gives you the right meaning for an erroneous reply.  Click ‘Review’ to see the correct meaning, definition, pronunciation, and sentences showing its proper usage.

2. Vocab Builder

Raise the game by putting your vocabulary to test through this app. Play ‘Match the Word’ section to find the correct answer for a given word. Mind you, only one option fits so choose carefully. Besides its ‘Match the Definition’ section, you can view the endless list of words and their meanings. This app is useful when you want to review your performance for SAT or GRE. The app can be downloaded from Google Play store for free.

3. Words, words, words

The ‘Learn’ feature shows words with definitions. The ‘Quiz’ feature lets you match a word to its right meaning. The feature lets you track your progress and time taken to solve the quiz. By default, the app is set to Beginner level but you can change the level of difficulty. The ‘Game’ feature offers scrambled words and their definition. Check its pronunciation by tapping on the megaphone icon.

4. Test your English Vocabulary

This app is essentially a useful tool for reviewing words that are appear in tests like TOEFL, GMAT, GRE, and TOEIC etc. Click on ‘Meaning By Word’ or ‘Word By Meaning’ to review the set of words. You can play games like Code Word, Anagram, Stop Word, Puzzle, and Millionaire. The app comes packed with Listening Tests.

5. SAT Vocabulary

Enough of challenges and vocabulary tests! Relax; we have SAT Vocabulary for you. The app shows words in white against a black backdrop. Tap ‘Next’ if you know the meaning of the word already. Otherwise flip or tap the word to check the correct meaning. It is a very basic and simple sort of app designed for SAT aspirants. The app is devoid of distracting ads to let you enjoy vocabulary building without interruptions.

6. Miss Spell’s Class

Matched words with meaning/definitions and unscrambled words! Do you actually know the correct spelling for the word? No! Then download Miss Spell’s Class app from Play Store and get going. Choose the correctly spelt words from the given set of 20 words. For every incorrect spelling your score receives a ten second penalty. ‘Review’ the quiz to learn the correct spellings. You can invite your pals from Twitter or Facebook to play spelling challenges.

7. English Vocabulary

Want stress free vocabulary learning? Download English Vocabulary on your mobile phone and start learning. The app initially introduces 25 words. Click on any word to see its meaning, pronunciation guide and usage. The ‘Read More’ link at the bottom of the page will connect you tot engvocabindia.blogspot.com where you can learn more about the word. Though the app is quite useful for learning new words, it does not provide any means to review your vocabulary expansion.

8. uVocab- Vocabulary Trainer

If you want to improve and build your vocabulary for an upcoming exam then this app offers an extensive stock of words that you can learn, listen to its pronunciation, search definitions online, or view its synonyms and usage. Check your growth through the ‘History’ feature.

Here are some of the Best NOTE taking APPS.

Evernote

Available across Android, iOS and windows, web and desktop platforms and is one of the most popular.

It is a modern workspace that syncs between all of your devices. Not just adding notes it also allows you to form pictures. There is a little microphone in the app that lets you record audio while you take notes, so that you can have reference for later. One can also attach files and discuss notes right on the APP. So start ‘Evernote’ing now!

With Papyrus you can write just like you would on paper using an active pen, passive stylus, or your finger. This app helps you take handwritten notes naturally on your Android tablet or phone!

One can also turn their device into a virtual whiteboard or give presentations in a class, meeting, or conference by wirelessly casting to a TV/projector (e.g. using Miracast, Chromecast). Export notes as PDFs or images, then share them with others or store them in the cloud!

Note Everything

Available on Android platform this app is a simple yet highly user-friendly notepad application which serves your basic purpose of taking notes without frills and fancies!

It also has additional features to record voice and paint-notes. Your notes can be organized in folders. You can create shortcuts on home, send notes, use live folders & much more!

Microsoft office OneNote

This is the most convenient for windows users. OneNote now has free apps for PC, Mac, Windows Phone, iPhone, iPad, and Android devices also.

A free Microsoft account is required to use OneNote. It is a digital notebook for your to-do lists, lecture and meeting notes, vacation plans, or anything you want to organize or remember. OneNote also saves pictures, audio files and converts your texts into PDF files.

Google Keep- notes and lists

This helps you quickly capture what’s on your mind and get a reminder later at the right place or time. It has a feature to speak a voice memo on the go and have it automatically transcribed. Also allows one to set a location-based reminder, share ideas with friends and family through photos and search and organize your notes the way you want!

Simple Note

Available both for Android and iOS, the app really keeps it simple! It is all about speed and efficiency. Open it, write some thoughts, and you’re done. As your collection of notes grows, you can search them instantly and keep them organized with tags and pins. You can also share notes and publish them for other people.

Penultimate

This app is exclusively for the iPad from Evernote. Penultimate is a digital handwriting app that combines the natural experience of pen and paper with power of Evernote’s sync and search features. If you want to explore your creative side, draw diagrams or sketches, this is the best app.

Fifty-three for iOS

From inception to launch, here’s how to start up while you’re still a student. Good luck!

Education is Education

The average e-learner—adults included—rarely carry the educative mindset associated with a formal classroom to a virtual classroom and this anomaly is largely due to the informal setting associated with learning from the comfort of your home or office space. Therefore the first and most difficult challenge that impedes the e-learner is that of the mind also known as ‘the lack of motivation.’

The first step to fighting ‘learning fatigue’ is understanding the reasons why you registered for a module or online courses. Was it by the behest of your parents or Boss? Or for your own personal development? The answer to these questions will go a long way in determining how beneficial the tips below will be to your educational pursuits. So please read on.

  1. Redefine Your Goals: Once you have determined your reason of participating in an online program, the next step is to define your studying strategy to meet these goals. Therefore if you plan on just passing to get the required certification, then your study goals should lean towards using test materials to prepare for corresponding exams while if you are in for personal development, creating study strategies that incorporate that education into your daily life is the tip that should be considered by you
  2. Create your Learning Culture: the phrase ‘learning timetable’ wasn’t used here for we all know how unruly those things are. Its replacement ‘learning culture’ means studying how your brain assimilates and tailoring all your learning activities around those assimilation periods. Therefore if you are a morning or evening person, it is important that you take advantage of your learning personality/period by ensuring that educational inclined tasks occur around your most efficient periods since e-learning gives you the needed time flexibility.
  3. Set Achievable Goals: I believe most students know or have heard about the benefits of setting small goals that can be achieved within a limited time duration and if you fall into the category of students in–the-know, it is really something you should consider integrating into your study schedule. The innovative tip here is to start an ‘achievable goals’ campaign—such as studying for a test or building an app/3Dmodel/circuit board etc.— having a time span under a month, focus on your strategy of achieving that goal and then crosschecking the results of your achievement against results gotten without the aid of a personal goal. The difference is surely destined to change your work ethic and approach to learning.
  4. Employ Collaborative Tools: the difficulties associated with having your course materials spread amongst diverse platforms—mail addresses, LMS, MOOC platforms etc.—and multiple devices—PC, tablets, phones having different operating systems—makes the task of learning a nightmare. Therefore to increase your productivity, it is essential that you make use of tools that collaborate the efforts of one another. Whether you are an Apple or Google fan boy/girl, the tip here is to ensure continuity between your devices and online accounts, so you can access your materials at any time using all your devices.
  5. Always Take Notes: Google Keep, Microsoft’s Stick Notes, Evernote, Samsung’s S Memo etc. are just a few of the note pads available to users of diverse devices looking to store information titbits for further research. The popularity of these productivity tools only goes to show that regardless of your career or professional line, there is always something new to learn each day and for students, the ‘something new’ to learn are usually much over a short period of time. Therefore keeping track of these new ideas and information while paying attention to your studies can easily be done using the electronic note pads synonymous to your devices.

In conclusion, it is important to note that every tip provided here is designed as a support system to the most important factors needed to achieve success in any field which are constantly practicing and making your educational goals one of central parts of your life.
